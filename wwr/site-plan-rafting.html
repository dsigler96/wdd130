Overview

Purpose

Hello and welcome to Walter's White Water Rafting! We are a family owned business that have been offering rafting instuction and tours for 30+ years throughout the Grand Canyon. Our team of experienced tour guides promise to take you on the ride of your life through the cannals of this wonderful Arizona location. We promise that we will thrill you on the edge of seat, so long as both arms and legs stay inside our quality, safety tested rafts. We want you to sit back, relax, maybe scream a little and enjoy the tour of a lifetime! So what are you waiting for? Take a ride on Walter's White Water Rafts!

Audience

Our target audience is an adventerous family. This white water rafting company offers "affordable" tours to middle class families looking for a fun get-away trip. Its fun for the whole family - the parents who have an itch to get out of their daily routines, the young children to go down rapids in a river, and even the younger children. We prioritize safety, so the danger is practically nonexistant so long as the crew follows the instructions of the tour guide. This website is meant to be accessed from a desktop computer, as it has reservation information and other various forms of contact, whether it be email or phone contacts.
